Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Overview

Red Mosquito is a fan-made Pearl Jam concert companion app. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require user accounts, registration, or login.

Local Storage

Concert attendance records (shows you mark as "I Was There") are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Network Requests

The app fetches read-only concert data (setlists, venues, songs, album information) from our backend database. These requests do not include any personal or identifying information.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at redmosquitoapp@proton.me.
